About Myself

Last year I read an authorised Elon Musk biography called “Elon Musk: Tesla, Space X, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future”. I was quite impressed by some of the things I learned about him, especially about his strong work ethics and unbelievable perseverance . It’s easy to look at all he’s achieved and assume that everything in his life mu st therefore be easy. His rockets successfully launching and returning, Tesla gett ing bigger and bigger, not to mention new evolving ventures from Hyperloop to the Boring company (and flamethrowers?). However, if you look at what he’s gone throu gh and how he overcame what others might have considered unbearable challenges, it really helps to get a bigger picture of him as a person. And you realise that there’s a price for everything.
